01 — The situation

The difficulty was never finding a building. It is everything you cannot see from the outside.

The title that looks clean and isn't. The licence that quietly decides what the asset is ever allowed to become. The tax position determined by how you buy, long before whether you profit. The agent who is warm, helpful, and working for the seller. The transaction that stalls in an office you have no way to reach — and the opportunity that moves on while it does.

You are expected to commit serious capital to irreversible decisions, in a system you cannot read and a language you do not negotiate in. Most foreign buyers manage this by trusting whoever happens to be in front of them, and hoping it holds.

That exposure is the thing I exist to remove.

02 — How I work

Three commitments — the ones that matter most when you cannot verify anything for yourself.

i

I act for you, and only you.

Not the seller, not the agent, not the developer who would prefer the deal close on their terms. My loyalty is single, and it is yours.

ii

One person, accountable from first question to final registration.

You are not handed between departments or left to assemble a deal from people who never speak to each other. One line of accountability runs through all of it. Mine.

iii

You always know where you stand.

No silence, no surprises at signing. You understand each decision before it is taken — explained in language meant to inform you, not to impress you.

03 — What you get

Not a list of services. A position that holds.

Acquisition, structured and closed.

Diligence that finds what matters. A holding structure decided before you commit, not after. The transaction taken cleanly through corporate setup, negotiation, notary and registry — without you chasing anyone.

Ownership that holds up.

Title, tax position and structure built to survive scrutiny — from the authorities, from a future buyer, and from your own succession.

Development and repositioning, de-risked.

For those who build, convert, or operate tourism assets: the licensing, planning and regulatory ground mapped before capital is committed — not discovered afterward.

A position you can leave.

Every structure is built with the eventual sale, refinancing or inheritance already in view.
