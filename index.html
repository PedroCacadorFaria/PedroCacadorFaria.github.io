You commit serious capital in a system you cannot read and a language you do not negotiate in.

The risk is rarely the building — it is the title that looks clean and isn't, the licence that limits what the asset can become, the tax fixed by how you buy. Removing that exposure is the work.

03 — Time

In Portugal, the deal is rarely lost on price. It is lost to delay.

Portuguese transactions move through notaries, registries and public offices that run on their own pace. A file left to that pace drifts — and an opportunity can move on while it waits.

My job is to keep the file moving. I prepare what each office needs before it asks, push the steps that can run in parallel, and stay on the parts that stall.
